About Us

Finding a reputable, experienced solicitor who will take a straightforward approach to resolving the legal issue you are facing is important.

At Bootes, we recognise the importance of having someone on your side.

Established in 1854, Bootes has an enviable and long-standing reputation as a quality firm for civil and commercial law matters. We are known for our legal abilities, and for being knowledgeable and solution-focused for our clients. We work to deliver quality, timely legal services and maintain a successful track record in assisting clients.

  • A Commercial Approach To Resolving Issues. Simply put, we consider all factors when representing our clients. Our Manchester business dispute solicitors understand and focus on the commercial position that our clients are in. We are proactive, driven and focused in achieving the right results for our clients.
  • Innovative Solutions, Straightforward Advice. We treat our clients how we would want to be treated and take a no-nonsense approach when resolving complex legal matters. You can depend on us for straightforward advice and a realistic view of what the possible outcomes are. Our solicitors are easy to communicate with, friendly and assertive in protecting clients' rights.

Areas of Service

The team at Bootes has a broad range of skills and abilities, and for each case and transaction we hone in on the most important legal issues at hand so we can address them directly.

We are commercial solicitors who handle cases and transactions that fall into four niche categories:

Dispute Resolution

Contract disputes; property disputes; shareholder & director disputes; injunctions; dilapidation claims and probate disputes.

Finance Litigation

Mortgage enforcement; unfair relationship actions; MCOB & CONC considerations; undue influence and regulatory features (including matters arising out of The Mortgage Credit Directive)

Property Finance

Drafting lending, security and guarantee documentation; acting in connection with transactions (including construction of houses; conversions of offices to residential units; refurbishment projects; auction purchases; custom-build projects and barn conversions); Section 106 agreements & unilateral undertakings; deeds of priority / subordination and assistance in the creation of management systems for lenders

Property

Commercial sales and purchases; lease transactions for landlords & tenants; overage agreements and deeds of easement

Dispute Resolution

Commercial disputes divert attention from your business, potentially threatening your daily operations and profitability. The experienced solicitors at Bootes have a strong background in commercial dispute resolution and will work diligently to assist you in reaching a favourable outcome.

We have a long-standing reputation and have been representing commercial and business clients in Manchester for more than 150 years. Our clients include entrepreneurs, high net-worth individuals, corporations, SME businesses, financial institutions and private equity capital providers who we have advised on a broad range of commercial disputes.

We have considerable experience in commercial law and a successful track record in working with business owners and professionals. Our firm has been successful in handling a number of Court of Appeal cases.

Financial Litigation

Our dispute resolution and property finance teams bring together specialist expertise from within the finance legal market that is second to none.

We have built up excellent relationships with and act for numerous financial institutions to include mainstream banks, secured loan providers and short-term finance houses. We have specialist knowledge in connection with finance & mortgage regulation (MCOB, MCD & CONC) and act for lenders who provide both regulated and unregulated products to their customers. We are perfectly placed to ensure that our clients treat their customers fairly whilst also ensuring that we achieve the required result for our clients.

Property Finance

Our niche property finance team includes specialists from within the bridging and development finance sector to offer experience and expertise that is unrivalled.

We act for financial institutions, including main-stream banks, secured loan providers and short-term bridging & development finance providers.

We deal with all types of lending including:

  • Mortgage loans regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority and governed by The Mortgages and Home Finance: Conduct of Business Sourcebook (MCOB)
  • Consumer Credit loans regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority and governed by the Consumer Credit Sourcebook (CONC) and the Consumer Credit Act
  • Loans exempted as for investment purposes, including buy to let loans
  • Corporate and non-corporate loans, including to companies, LLPs, partnerships and charities

Property

With a wealth of experience in acting for sellers and buyers, landlords and tenants, our niche specialist property team has the understanding, knowledge and know-how to help you navigate the hurdles and pitfalls of any transaction

We advise on all aspects of property. Why not give us a call on 0161 832 7888 or email info@bootes.co.uk to find out how we can help you.
